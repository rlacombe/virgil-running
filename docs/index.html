What it does

Your training data,
through the lens of science

Switchback connects to your Intervals.icu account and helps you think through your training by anchoring every recommendation in established exercise science.

Review a workout. Flag a recovery concern. Adjust your week. Build a race-day plan. All through natural conversation.

switchback

Not a coach.

Switchback is a companion: presenting your data, explaining tradeoffs, and letting you decide. When you can work with a real coach, you absolutely should. Switchback is for the many of us who train alone and want something better than a static plan.

This project is for informational and educational purposes only. It is not medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before starting or modifying a training program. Stay safe out there.

Make it yours

Your companion. Your rules.

During setup, you choose a name and personality for your companion. The default is Virgil — Dante's faithful companion on his journey to hell and back. Rename it after a training partner, a runner you admire, or that aid station volunteer who saved your race at mile 40.

You also choose the tone, humor, and style that work for you. Casual or structured. Brief or detailed. Fired up or analytical. It adapts to how you want to train.
